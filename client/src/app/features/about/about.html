How does LoFu work?

1

Snap and Share

Whether you've lost an item or found something that doesn't belong to you, posting takes less than a minute. Just upload a photo, add a location tag, and write a brief description.

2

Explore the feed.

Users can scroll through the latest "Lost" and "Found" posts in their area to see if a matching item has been reported. Our simple layout makes it easy to spot what you're looking for.

3

Send a message.

If you recognize an item, just click the "I found it!" button. This starts a private conversation where you can coordinate a safe handover and get the item back home.

Our History

LoFu was born from a simple observation of our local community in Angeles City. We noticed that while our neighborhoods are full of helpful people, there wasn't a dedicated, organized space to reconnect lost belongings with their rightful owners.

We created LoFu to turn that stress into relief by providing a transparent, easy-to-use platform where neighbors can look out for one another.
